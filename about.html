About Eos

Eos is a coalition to restore minds and soil—uniting educators, scientists, farmers, builders, and communities.

We design place-based education that ties math and science to real landscapes; food and health to the school day; and skilled trades to workforce pathways. Our pilots are evaluated with university partners and published as open toolkits for districts.
